FAQ

What is EcoOpti and how does its AI work to save at scale?

EcoOpti is an AI-driven solution for smart e-commerce packaging. Its advanced machine-learning algorithm analyzes each product’s dimensions, shipping method, and your sustainability preferences in real time. It then recommends the perfect box size and eco-friendly material (recycled cardboard, mushroom packaging, compostable mailers, etc.), reducing waste by up to 30%, lowering shipping costs, and improving your ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) metrics with 99% accuracy on every order.

How much can EcoOpti realistically save my business on packaging and shipping costs?

Customers typically report average savings of around 30% on combined packaging materials and shipping fees. Actual savings depend on factors like your order volume, existing packaging practices, and product mix. In most cases, businesses see a minimum 20% cost reduction within the first month. As soon as the AI goes live, you’ll notice immediate decreases in both material usage and postage expenses.

Can EcoOpti integrate seamlessly with my e-commerce store (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento)

Yes. EcoOpti offers no-code integrations for major e-commerce platforms such as Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, and BigCommerce, plus a flexible API for custom setups. Initial setup takes under 10 minutes: simply connect your store account, grant access to product and shipping data, and the AI starts optimizing automatically—no technical expertise required.

What types of packaging materials does EcoOpti support or recommend?

EcoOpti supports a wide range of eco-friendly materials, including: Recycled and Virgin Cardboard – Balances strength and sustainability. Biodegradable Plastics – Ideal for lightweight or moisture-sensitive items. Compostable Mailers – Break down naturally after use. Mushroom Packaging – A cutting-edge, biodegradable alternative to Styrofoam. Our AI picks the best material based on product weight, dimensions, and your ESG targets. You can also set preferences like “minimal plastic only” or “prioritize 100% organic materials.”

How does EcoOpti help with ESG reporting and showcasing sustainability to investors and customers?

EcoOpti generates comprehensive analytics on: Waste Reduction – Exact volume and units of packaging saved per SKU. CO₂ Emissions Savings – Estimated reduction in carbon footprint for each shipment.
